Trend Towards Equality

Change in Global Gender Gap Index (2006-2013)

The UN Humanitarian Data Exchange scored the gender gap index in countries worldwide according to several factors. Factors include educational attainment, economic participation, and political empowerment. Here, the data are depicted in terms of overall scores. A score of 1 corresponds to gender equality while a score of 0 corresponds to inequality.

The gender gap index score decreased in countries depicted in red. The countries in blue experienced an increase in their gender gap index score, with light blue representing the smallest increase and dark blue representing the greatest increase.

Source: New York State Department of Education, EngageNY.org
